Dear Parent

Welcome to Extramarks Live.

As a free subscriber you have access to the following services:

  • All academic (under the categories of Learn, Practice and Test) and enabling services (Progress, Scheduler, Group, note and Mentor) for one chapter from all subjects for the board and class mentioned in the profile of the first child specified by you.
  • All academic services under the category of Learn and Practice for the first chapters of all subjects for all Boards and classes.

Your status as a parent entitles you to the following:

  • You can be a mentor to anyone else registered as a student. The student needs to send you an invitation to activate this service.
  • You may check the progress section for the chapters available for view to your child.

We wish you a happy and enriching experience at Extramarks Live.
